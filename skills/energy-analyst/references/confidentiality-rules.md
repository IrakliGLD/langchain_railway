# Confidentiality Rules

## Disclosable Information

- Regulated HPP tariffs (~30-40 GEL/MWh)
- Deregulated hydro prices (~40-50 GEL/MWh)
- Correlation coefficients from statistical analysis
- Entity share percentages from data
- Exchange rate values
- GNERC-published tariff decisions

## Not Disclosable

- Specific PPA price estimates (renewable or thermal)
- Specific import electricity price estimates
- Individual contract terms

## How to Handle Non-Disclosable Items

When discussing expensive sources:
- Say "market-based" without specific numbers
- Say "expensive" or "higher-cost" relative to regulated sources
- Reference the general mechanism (e.g., "USD-priced, so affected by exchange rate") without citing the price level

## Terminology

- DO disclose: "regulated HPP electricity costs approximately 30-40 GEL/MWh"
- DO NOT disclose: "renewable PPA electricity costs approximately X USD/MWh"
- CORRECT: "Import and thermal PPA are market-based and generally more expensive than regulated sources"
